About / Purpose

This project helps people in need, especially children and women. We provide education, essentials, and opportunities for growth.

Our focus is on Butuan City, Mindanao, Philippines. We aim to give people chances to improve their lives safely and responsibly.

“Count on Me” reminds us that small acts of care ripple outward. Every donation here becomes a lifeline, guiding children, women, and families toward brighter paths. Listen on Spotify. We do not own this song by Bruno Mars.
